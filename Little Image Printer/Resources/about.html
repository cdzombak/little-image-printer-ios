Little Image Printer first came to life at the Little Printer Hack Day hosted by BERG on 2nd March 2013. Put together in around five and a half hours, the first cut was a little rough around the edges, but was completed in time to demo at the end of the day. Since then, the app has been tidied, polished a little and made a little more presentable, ready for release to the world.

If you're a developer and want to take a look behind the scenes, all of the source code for this build can be found over on github at github.com/cdzombak/little-image-printer-ios.
